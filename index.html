Hangeul

The Korean Alphabet

About

Hangul is the writing system of the Korean language. Hangul is made up of 14 consonants and 10 vowels, making it an alphabet with a total of 24 letters. It is the official writing system in South Korea and North Korea (where it is known as Chos≈èn muntcha), and it is used by diaspora Koreans across the world. Many people say it is one of the easist and most fun languages to learn - so let's do just that now and pick up a few words while we're at it!
